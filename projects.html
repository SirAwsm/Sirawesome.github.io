How to power your robot

  • How to power a robot

    There are two ways to power a robot. The first way is to use a charger. Some robots have a plug that you can plug into a socket, like a normal charger. The other way is to use batteries. You can buy the batteries from stores in Hong Kong. The type of battery used depends on what the robot requires. The battery needed is printed on the box.
