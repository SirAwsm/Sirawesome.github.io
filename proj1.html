How to control your robot

Drone

The two main ways to control a robot is by remote control and by phone. Some robots come with a remote control that lets you control the robot. Some of the remote controls have to downloaded onto your phone to be used. This is used for some drone controls, as it can let you see what the drone is seeing from the phone. Some robots are voice controlled. They can listen to what you are saying, process it in the conputer chip and do what you want them to do. These robots are harder to buy and are a lot more expensive.
